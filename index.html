JAMB CBT — General Instructions

  • 1This is a Computer-Based Test (CBT). Your answers are recorded automatically as you click each option.
  • 2The examination starts immediately when you click "Start Examination". The timer counts down from your set time limit.
  • 3Use the question palette to navigate. Green = answered, Yellow = flagged, Grey = unanswered.
  • 4You may flag any question to revisit later using the flag button on each question.
  • 5Use of English has 60 questions covering: Comprehension Passages, Cloze Test, Lexis & Structure, Oral English, and Reading Text (The Lekki Headmaster). Passages are shown above each question.
  • 6A scientific calculator is available via the ⊞ button at the bottom-right corner (useful for Mathematics/Further Maths).
  • 7When satisfied with your answers, click "Submit Exam" to see your score. You can review all answers after submission.
  • 8The exam ends automatically when the timer reaches zero. All saved answers are submitted.
